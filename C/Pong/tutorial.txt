A ping pong console game. 

A game for two players. 
Control: 
1st player - up "a", down "z"; 
2nd player - up "k", down "m". 

The task of the players is to score a goal and not miss the ball =)

Compile the game in CLI:
gcc -Wall -Wextra -Werror pong.c

Open file in CLI:
./ a.out
